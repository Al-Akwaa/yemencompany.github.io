Welcome to Yemen Company

(Fahem Group) is the parent company of Yemen Company for Industrial Investment Ltd. Fahem Group was founded in 1935. The main business of the company is the import of soft commodities such as wheat, sugar, rice, flour, palm oil and sunflower oil. The Fahem Group has also recently begun importing gasoil. The head office of the company is based in Hodeidah in which the company has a long lease on a port called Salif on the Red Sea just north of the city. At the port there are directly alongside the berth discharging machines, 100,000 metric tonnes of silos and a flour mill which can process 1,500 metric tonnes of wheat a day. The staple diet of the Yemeni nation of 30 million is bread and 90% of the requirement is imported into the Yemen.

Services

The group began activities in 1945 by setting up two production lines of ghee and palm oil. Then the group expanded its operation into grain trading as well as all soft commodities such as rice and sugar.

Grain Vessel Discharging

Our grain unloaders can discharge grain and other agri-bulk commodities at rated capacities up to 1,800t/h. Our lightweight, compact belt-type grain loaders offer capacities of up to 2,000t/h for ships up to post-Panamax size. Our efficient, clean conveying systems can be matched to our grain ship loaders and ship unloaders. Our grain handling systems provide exceptional environment-friendly performance with minimal dust and no spillage.

Wheat Flour Milling

Our flour mill can process up to 2000 metric tonnes of wheat a day with an expansion under construction to 4500 metric tonnes of wheat a day. Hard wheat flour is the main food ingredient in bread and baking products due to its good milling and baking characteristics. It is produced by separating the wheat kernel into its three parts: bran (the outer protective covering), germ (the new plant embryo), and endosperm (the tissue that provides nutrients to the germ). Endosperm is the part of the wheat kernel that is finely milled into wheat flour. .

Grain Trading

Through the year, we carefully source all classes of wheat from trusted farms across major exporting regions such as the Black Sea, Europe, Australia, North America and South America. Then they are shipped to our storage facilities in the Port of Salif –Hodeidah/Yemen & Aden. .

Construction Material Trading

We are in business of construction materials supply for the construction projects of any scale. Our line of products ranges from basic raw construction materials to the products for interior work. That is to say, we are trading in cement and steel construction materials for our own projects and the local yemeni market. We carefully select our partners to ensure the top quality of all products. Our manufacturers and distributors are trusted and reputable companies.
